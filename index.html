I am a tenure-tack assistant professor at Gaoling School of AI, Renmin University of China. I am leading the machine learning group in RUC. My research interests are primarily on deep generative models, approximate Bayesian inference, and their applications with limtied supervision and limited data.

Before joining RUC, I was a Post Doc researcher in TSAIL at Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Jun Zhu. I recieved my Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Bo Zhang. During my Ph.D., I had spent a great year at AMLab, working with Prof. Max Welling. I received the B.E. degree from Institute for Interdiscriplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University in 2014.

Positions available for self-motivated Ph.D and master students. :-)
